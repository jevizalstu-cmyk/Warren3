What is Wuthering Waves?

Wuthering Waves is a visually captivating open-world action RPG set in a world ravaged by a mysterious calamity known as the Lament. You awaken as the Rover — a silent, amnesiac protagonist drawn into a land teeming with the remnants of lost civilizations and dangerous anomalies. The game offers a beautifully decayed world to explore, rich with hidden lore, intense boss fights, and fluid, high-speed traversal. Players can engage in stylish combat while uncovering the forgotten truths of the world and their own identity.

Featured Character: Rover

Rover is the central character in Wuthering Waves — a wanderer from beyond who holds the key to the world's salvation or its ruin. Unlike traditional heroes, Rover’s identity and power grow alongside the narrative, shaped by the choices players make and the memories they recover. With the ability to absorb and wield Resonator powers, Rover’s combat style is adaptive, offering dynamic synergy with the party. Their journey is personal, poetic, and intimately tied to the fate of the post-Lament world.

Combat & Exploration

Wuthering Waves delivers a deeply satisfying gameplay loop combining high-mobility exploration with skill-based combat. Players can double-jump, wall-run, glide, and grapple across vertical environments, giving them complete freedom to traverse a shattered world. Combat is fast, combo-driven, and cinematic — emphasizing precision, timing, and strategy. Each Resonator has unique elemental abilities and stylish animations, encouraging team synergy and creative tactics in battle. The world itself reacts to your presence, creating an immersive experience that evolves as you play.
